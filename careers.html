1. Software Developer

Location: Remote
Job Description:
Azayd IT Consultancy is seeking a talented and passionate Software Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in software development, particularly in web and mobile applications. Responsibilities include designing, coding, testing, and deploying software solutions that meet client requirements. You will work closely with our project managers and clients to deliver high-quality, scalable, and maintainable software. Proficiency in programming languages such as JavaScript, Python, or Java, and experience with frameworks like React, Angular, or Django is essential. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work in an agile environment are also crucial. Join us to contribute to innovative projects and advance your career in a supportive and collaborative environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and deploy software solutions
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define project requirements
  • Maintain and improve existing software applications
  • Write clean, scalable, and efficient code
  • Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback


    • Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
  • 3+ years of experience in software development
  • Proficiency in JavaScript, Python, or Java
  • Experience with frameworks such as React, Angular, or Django
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail


    • 2. IT Project Manage

    Location: New York, NY
    Job Description:
    Azayd IT Consultancy is looking for an experienced IT Project Manager to lead and manage various IT projects for our clients. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of successfully delivering projects on time, within scope, and within budget. You will be responsible for planning, executing, and finalizing projects according to strict deadlines and within budget. This includes acquiring resources and coordinating the efforts of team members and third-party contractors or consultants to deliver projects according to plan. The IT Project Manager will also define the project’s objectives and oversee quality control throughout its life cycle.

    Key Responsibilities:

  • Define project scope, goals, and deliverables
  • Develop detailed project plans and schedules
  • Coordinate internal resources and third parties/vendors for project execution
  • Manage project budget and resource allocation
  • Track project performance and report to senior management
  • Ensure all projects are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget


    • Qualifications:
  • Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field
  • 5+ years of project management experience in the IT sector
  • PMP or PRINCE2 certification is preferred
  • Strong understanding of project management methodologies
  • Excellent communication and leadership skills
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
